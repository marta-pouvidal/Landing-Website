What is Maternal Motion?

MATERNAL MOTION is an innovative sportswear belt equipped with an ultrasound transducer used to detect fetal heartbeat during prenatal care in its basic version. Maternal Motion redefines the experience of pregnancy for active women. It addresses a common concern many expectant mothers face: the fear that engaging in exercise or continuing their normal lifestyle might harm their baby. This innovative belt empowers women to embrace life and stay active, dispelling worries with its advanced, constant monitoring technology. With Maternal Motion, mothers-to-be can "Just Do It," enjoying peace of mind and the freedom to live their lives without compromise, confident in the safety and well-being of their baby.
